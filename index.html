Peanut Butter Sandwich Recipe

  1. Get two slices of bread, peanut butter, and any optional toppings like jelly, honey, or banana slices.
  2. Place the slices of bread on a flat surface, like a plate or cutting board.
  3. Use a butter knife to evenly spread peanut butter on one side of one slice of bread.
  4. If desired, spread jelly, drizzle honey, or layer banana slices on top of the peanut butter.
  5. Place the second slice of bread on top, press gently, and enjoy your sandwich!
