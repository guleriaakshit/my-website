About Me

Hi, I'm Akshit Guleria, a passionate individual from Himachal Pradesh, exploring the intersection of technology, design, and creativity.

From growing up amidst the serene mountains to diving into web development, my journey has been a constant learning experience.

I enjoy working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, and I believe technology should be intuitive and accessible for everyone.

On this website, I share my personal experiences, lessons learned, and upcoming projects.

I also write about life in Himachal and how the beauty of nature influences my work.

My goal is to continuously improve my skills, contribute to the tech community, and create user-centered solutions.

Feel free to connect with me for collaboration, discussions, or any exciting projects!
